Project statement

My overall vision is to collaborate with athletes and anyone with expertise in a performance art to create images that convey the sensation of freedom through movement, particularly in wild places. The context of the body of work is ideally in remote, or at least wildly inspiring, locations mainly with natural lighting. Some off-camera flash may be used to create dynamic lighting effects. Slow shutter speed may be used to introduce more dynamism of the subject with the higher aim of generating more authentic moments compared to staged stills. A large amount of the material is likely to be in black and white. I'm also starting a very similar art nude project.

General shoot topics

  • Dance
  • Bouldering
  • Running
  • Backcountry ice skating
  • Powder skiing
  • Slacklining
  • Surfing (*)
  • Biking
  • Free diving (*)
  • Yoga
  • Horseback riding
Open to other activities, so if you have an idea please reach out.

(*) Would require renting extra equipment on my end and likely some test runs before the actual shoot.

In general, I'll aim to provide mood boards well before the shoot so as to establish the desired overall aesthetic.

Compensation:

Generally, I can only do TFP (time for photos). For particular shoots we can discuss moderate compensation but that requires exceptional circumstances. If you're interested in modeling for an art nude project (can be implied and/or anonymous), monetary compensation is possible.
