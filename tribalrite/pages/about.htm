Sean makes digital images as a creative outlet. Painting, drawing, and more broadly mixed media served as a creative outlet growing up, but through his outdoor experiences, his father's photographic influences, and a fast-moving world, he has transitioned to primarily using photography for artistic self-expression. Born and raised in the foothills of the Sierra Nevada, Sean enjoys playing and photographing in big landscapes. You may reach me with questions or comments at: info [at] seanandersonphotography.com
